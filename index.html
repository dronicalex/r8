ABOUT US
Lightgive Capital is a foundation specializing in investing in promising start-up crypto projects. Our foundation provides the opportunity to finance successful crypto projects in the early stages of their development to investors with a relatively small deposits. The main values of Lightgive Capital are the following: trust and loyalty of the investors, high-quality service, honesty, maximum transparency, and individual approach to each investor. Lightgive Capital's goal is to maximize the success of the companies in our portfolio
